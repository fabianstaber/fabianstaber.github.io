## Why Become a Member?

Speedcubing Austria is a volunteer-run association dedicated to promoting speedcubing in Austria. As a member, your membership fee supports us in organizing competitions. Additionally, you have the opportunity to actively participate in the association and vote in the general assembly.

## How to Become a Member?

To become a member, please download the membership form, fill it out, and send it to [vorstand@speedcubingaustria.at](mailto:vorstand@speedcubingaustria.at). You can also find our statutes for download.
